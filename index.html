Welcome to Couples Resort & Spa

5 Star Luxury in the Beautiful Muskoka, Ontario

First Class, Romantic Getaway, Couples Only

Welcome to Couples Resort, 5 Star Luxury Resort, which sits on the shores of Galeairy Lake. Whether you need to celebrate or need a rejuvenating break, to indulge in a romantic getaway or to be pampered in the Spa or to participate in endless activities & adventures in a Signature Canadian Experience, the world famous, Algonquin Park.

Award Winning Organic Spa

Escape and rejuvenate yourself and your loved one with a spa vacation. With individual options ranging from Massage Therapy, Hot Stones, Manicures/Pedicures, Facials and more, as well as Couples Resort exclusive packages, let us relax and revitalize you and your partner after an incredible day of activities and exhilaration.

Couples Activities

Since this is a couples resort, every activity has been tailored to a romantic day with your partner. Enjoy incredible hikes and bike rides through the scenic Algonquin Park hiking trails. Enjoy a relaxing row off the shores of the Galeiary Lake, taking in the bountiful nature and explore the gorgeous waterfall. Take a romantic ride on horseback throughout horse trails in the wilderness. Couples Resort also has seasonal activites as well, such as snowboarding, ice skating, swimming, autumn wildlife viewing, moose watching and so much more.

First Class Dining Experience

Start everyday exciting, and what better way to do that than with an incredible breakfast. We have table served, as well as in room breakfast delivery, with options such as seasonal fresh fruit, eggs benedict with bacon, and hollandaise on an english muffin, and more. Of course, no romantic getaway is made without a 5 course, first class dinner selection. Enjoy starters like Tandoori Prawns, entrees like Rack of Lamb or Duck Breast, desserts like White Chocolate Cheesecake, an Amouse Bouche, wine from our award winning cellar (or bring your own) and so many more choices, you and your partner will never want to return home and cook again. And of course, live entertainment every night.
